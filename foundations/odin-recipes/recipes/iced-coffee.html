Iced Coffee

Recipes

To make a refreshing iced coffee, start by brewing a strong cup of coffee using your preferred method. Once brewed, let it cool to room temperature or chill it in the refrigerator for quicker results. Fill a glass with ice cubes and pour the cooled coffee over the ice. Add your desired amount of milk or cream, and sweeten to taste with sugar, simple syrup, or a flavored syrup like vanilla or caramel. Stir well and enjoy your homemade iced coffee, perfect for a quick and energizing treat on a warm day.

Ingredients

  • Strong brewed coffee
  • Ice cubes
  • Milk or cream (optional)
  • Sugar or simple syrup (optional)
  • Flavored syrup (e.g., vanilla, caramel) (optional)

Steps

  1. Brew a strong cup of coffee using your preferred method.
  2. Allow the coffee to cool to room temperature, or chill it in the refrigerator for quicker results.
  3. Fill a glass with ice cubes.
  4. Pour the cooled coffee over the ice.
  5. Add your desired amount of milk or cream, if using.
  6. Sweeten to taste with sugar or simple syrup, if desired.
  7. Optionally, add a flavored syrup like vanilla or caramel for extra flavor.
  8. Stir well to combine all the ingredients.
  9. Enjoy your homemade iced coffee!
